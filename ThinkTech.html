Aadhaar Linked ≠ DBT Enabled

What is Aadhaar Linked?

Your Aadhaar is recorded at your bank. This is an identity connection only.

  • Aadhaar recorded in bank account details
  • Used for KYC & identification
  • Doesn't guarantee govt transfers

What is DBT-enabled (Aadhaar-seeded)?

Your Aadhaar is mapped in payment networks (NPCI) to allow Direct Benefit Transfer.

  • Required for scholarship DBT disbursement
  • If missing → scholarship delays
  • Students must ensure seeding is active

How to check DBT status

1
Go to the bank/NPCI website or use the 'Aadhaar mapper' option (ask your bank if you can't find it).
2
For security, portals often ask for only the last 4 digits to show status.
3
Status will show 'Active' / 'Inactive' or similar. If inactive, follow action steps below or contact bank.
Important: Do not share your full Aadhaar number publicly. Use official portals or visit your bank branch.
